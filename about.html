About Sirius Minerals Holdings

Sirius Minerals Holdings is a group holding company comprised of:

Sirius Minerals Holdings (South Africa)

Registered in South Africa as the holding company and subsidiary of Khumo Mineral Brokers & Consultants - overseeing strategy, governance, and global reach (Sirius MTP).

Sirius Minerals & Precious Stones Ltd (Zambia)

Registered in Zambia as the operational arm managing supplier relationships, local compliance, and trade execution (Sirius MTP).

Mission

To formalise, digitise, and unlock the mineral trade ecosystem in Africa, starting with Zambia & South Africa, enabling transparency, compliance, and shared value for miners, buyers, and regulators.

Vision

A fully formalised, technology-driven mineral marketplace where ASM and small-scale miners can trade on equal footing with transparent pricing, accessible financing, and strong compliance.

Our Pillars

  • Compliance & Responsible Sourcing
  • Inclusive Growth for ASM
  • Digital Infrastructure & Data
  • Market Access & Liquidity
  • Cross-border Enablement

Governance & Risk

We implement layered KYC/KYB, AML, and regulatory compliance workflows across our entire trade stack. Every transaction is traceable, document-backed, and auditable.

Work with us

We collaborate with regulators, buyers, financial institutions, and cooperatives.
