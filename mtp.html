Sirius MTP – Mineral Trade, Formalised.

The Sirius Mineral Trade Platform is an end-to-end digital system for compliant mineral sourcing, pricing, contracting, documentation, logistics, and settlement.

Core Capabilities

Everything required to trade minerals compliantly and efficiently.

Onboarding & KYC/KYB

Automated identity, permit, and license verification for miners, sellers, buyers, and agents.

Deal Registration & Contracts

Digitally register deals, auto-generate contracts, escrow instructions, and KPIs.

Compliance & ESG

Built-in regulatory flows, reporting, and audit trails for authorities and partners.

Logistics & Documentation

Automated doc packs (assay, export, transport), milestone tracking, and alerts.
